Beach Driving Tips

First time driving on Nantucket's beaches? We want you to succeed. Here are the essentials:

  • Deflate your tires 15 PSI standard, 12 PSI for soft sand
  • Use 4WD 4HI normally, 4LO when it gets soft
  • Maintain momentum Keep moving—stopping in soft sand is risky
