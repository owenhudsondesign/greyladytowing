About Grey Lady Towing

Named after Nantucket itself, we're as much a part of this island as the fog.

Our Story

Grey Lady Towing isn't just a business name—it's a tribute to the island we call home. "The Grey Lady" has been Nantucket's nickname for centuries, inspired by the characteristic fog that blankets our shores. When thick fog rolled in during early mornings and late evenings, sailors would say they were heading to "The Grey Lady."

With over 20 years as part of Nantucket's year-round community, we've seen this island through every season. We've watched summer visitors discover our beaches for the first time, and we've helped locals navigate the quieter months when the crowds go home.

We started Grey Lady Towing because we saw a need: fair, fast, reliable beach recovery from people who actually know this island. Not a mainland company dispatching from miles away, but neighbors helping neighbors.
